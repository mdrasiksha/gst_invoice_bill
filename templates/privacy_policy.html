Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GST Smart collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our GST invoice generation platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information needed to create accounts, manage customers, generate invoices, improve product reliability, and provide support.

Account Information

Account details may include your name, business name, email address, password credentials, GSTIN, address, and other company profile information you provide.

Invoice Data

Invoice data may include customer details, line items, tax details, invoice numbers, payment information, logos, QR codes, and e-signatures uploaded or entered by you.

Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, protect sessions, remember preferences, and understand basic application usage.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security, so you are responsible for maintaining strong passwords and device security.

Third Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for hosting, storage, analytics, email, payments, or infrastructure. These providers process information only as needed to deliver their services.

User Rights

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal, tax, accounting, fraud-prevention, and legitimate business retention requirements.

Contact Information

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at gstsmartsupport@gmail.com.
